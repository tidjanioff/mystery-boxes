Game Rules

Welcome to Mystery Boxes™ !
Your mission is to find all hidden on a grid map. Each box on the grid contains a number that indicates how many hidden pieces are adjacent to it, including those touching by sides or corners, with no more than one piece per box. Use the displayed numbers to deduce the locations of the hidden pieces and mark your guesses carefully and strategically. You win by successfully identifying all the hidden pieces with no more than three incorrect guesses.
